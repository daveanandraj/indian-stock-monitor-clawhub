# Data sources — priority order

## Primary (regulatory / official — trust these most)
- **NSE corporate announcements**: https://www.nseindia.com/companies-listing/corporate-filings-announcements
- **BSE corporate announcements**: https://www.bseindia.com/corporates/ann.html
- **SEBI orders & news**: https://www.sebi.gov.in/sebiweb/home/HomeAction.do?doListing=yes&sid=1&ssid=6&smid=0
- **NSE surveillance (ASM/GSM)**: https://www.nseindia.com/regulations/asm-listed-securities
- **BSE surveillance**: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/equity/EQReports/Surveillance.aspx

## Fundamentals & filings
- **Screener.in**: https://www.screener.in/company/{TICKER}/ — quarterly results, ratios, shareholding, concall transcripts
- **Trendlyne**: https://trendlyne.com/equity/Snapshot/{TICKER}/ — consensus estimates, broker ratings
- **TIKR / Tijori**: alternate fundamentals
- **Annual reports**: company IR page

## Concall transcripts & investor presentations
- Screener "Documents" tab (often fastest)
- Company investor relations page
- Researchbytes (paid)

## News (use multiple, cross-check)
- **Moneycontrol**: https://www.moneycontrol.com/india/stockpricequote/{slug}
- **Economic Times Markets**: https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/markets
- **Business Standard**: https://www.business-standard.com/markets
- **Mint**: https://www.livemint.com/market
- **Reuters India**: https://www.reuters.com/world/india/
- **Bloomberg Quint / NDTV Profit**: https://www.ndtvprofit.com/

## Brokerage views & ratings
- Trendlyne broker ratings page
- Moneycontrol broker recommendations
- Smart Investor / Capital Market

## Credit ratings
- **CRISIL**: https://www.crisil.com/
- **ICRA**: https://www.icra.in/
- **CARE Ratings**: https://www.careratings.com/
- **India Ratings (Fitch)**: https://www.indiaratings.co.in/

## Block / bulk deals
- NSE bulk deals: https://www.nseindia.com/market-data/large-deals
- BSE bulk deals: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/equity/EQReports/bulk_deals.aspx

## Pre-market global cues
- SGX Nifty / GIFT Nifty: ET Markets pre-market page
- US close: any major source
- Brent crude, USD/INR, Dow futures: Investing.com

## Insider trading / SAST
- NSE: corporate filings → SAST/PIT
- BSE: insider trading disclosures

## Mutual fund holdings
- ValueResearch, Trendlyne MF holdings, MorningStar India

## Sector-specific
- **Banks**: RBI weekly statistical supplement, financial stability report
- **Auto**: SIAM monthly sales, FADA retail data
- **Cement**: company monthly volume disclosures, ICRA cement reports
- **IT**: NASSCOM, peer concall reads (Infosys/TCS/HCLT)
- **Pharma**: USFDA inspection observations (Form 483, Warning Letters), DCGI
- **Realty**: ANAROCK / Knight Frank quarterly reports
- **FMCG**: Nielsen / NielsenIQ category data, monsoon reports
- **Power/Energy**: CEA daily reports, oil ministry data

## Macro
- RBI press releases & MPC minutes
- Budget documents
- CMIE / MOSPI data releases
